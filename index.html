About Us

BFPC is your destination for quality education and expert consultancy services.

Whether you are looking to learn new skills or need professional advice, we've got you covered.

Bright Future Prabidhik Shikshalaya (BFPS) is a modern educational institution in the Mid-West region of Lumbini Province. This Institute is located in Dang District Ghorahi Sub-Metropolitan City 15 Bharatpur, Nepal. It was established in the year 2021 A.D to conduct technical, practical and short term training dedicated to meet the growing need for quality practical education. BFPS sprouted in the educational field with the support of academically conscious parents and the efforts of social service oriented people with the aim of providing quality technical and practical education in the field of modern education, with the aim of providing quality technical and practical education with excellence and strict discipline has been established to provide with a team of committed and experienced trainers and dedicated teaching and non-teaching staff, the academy aims to make the institution a center of excellence by providing high quality academic services along with related academic activities.

In survey strategy, we emerge as the top marking institute in terms of academic excellence, placement witness, infrastructural facilities, well-managed faculty, and many more. Our total program is based on a plan that can propel the candidate to a higher level of success. We know that students face many difficulties in choosing the right career path to complete their studies and excel. Since its inception, this educational institution has been conducting computer training for free and with special discounts targeting Nepali youths who are economically disadvantaged, poor, Dalit, helpless, disabled and marginalized. Shikshalaya has also been conducting short term trainings in various livelihoods targeting marginalized communities and needy people.
